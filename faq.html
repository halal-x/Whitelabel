Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our white-label solution, licensing, and compliance.

Does HalalX provide legal or regulatory licenses? +
No. HalalX provides the software infrastructure (SaaS) only. Obtaining the necessary financial or Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) licenses in your target country is entirely your responsibility as the operating entity.
How long does it take to launch? +
Depending on the plan and customization required, a beta staging environment can be ready in 3 to 4 weeks. Full mainnet launch usually takes 6 to 8 weeks after final configurations and KYC integrations.
Do you provide liquidity for the exchange? +
Yes, we provide institutional-grade liquidity bridges (via API) to top global exchanges (e.g., Binance, Kraken) to ensure your order books are deep and active from day one, while strictly maintaining spot-only execution rules.
Can we add our own custom coin or token? +
Yes, you can list your custom ERC-20, BEP-20, or native tokens. However, to maintain the platform's integrity, our compliance team must verify that the token's utility does not violate Islamic finance principles before integrating it into the main engine.
