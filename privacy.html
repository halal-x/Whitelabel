Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use HalalX Technologies' website or contact our sales team, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, company name, and any details you provide in your communications with us.
  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about how visitors interact with our website (e.g., pages visited, time spent, location).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly for B2B communication and service improvement:

  • To respond to your inquiries regarding our white-label solutions.
  • To send technical proposals, pricing details, and project timelines.
  • To analyze website traffic and improve user experience.

3. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. All data is stored securely. Your inquiries are handled directly by our internal technical sales team. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. End-User Data (For our Clients)

HalalX provides the software infrastructure to our clients. We do not directly collect, process, or store the personal data (including KYC documents) of the end-users who trade on our clients' exchanges. Data privacy compliance for end-users is the sole responsibility of the client operating the exchange.
